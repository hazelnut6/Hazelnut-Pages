The Girl With the Dragon Tattoo ($15.99)

Wishlist     Add to cart

Summary:


It's about Mikael Blomkvist, a crusading journalist recently at the wrong end of a libel case, hired to get to the bottom of Harriet's disappearance . . . and about Lisbeth Salander, a twenty-four-year-old pierced and tattooed genius hacker possessed of the hard-earned wisdom of someone twice her age—and a terrifying capacity for ruthlessness to go with it—who assists Blomkvist with the investigation. This unlikely team discovers a vein of nearly unfathomable iniquity running through the Vanger family, astonishing corruption in the highest echelons of Swedish industrialism—and an unexpected connection between themselves.





Book Reviews

Sally⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

It is one of the best books that I read. Initially, when I was reading this novel, I did not like this novel. But as soon as I read it, I started getting interested in this novel. if you understand its symbolic meaning and the depth of its themes. You have to look deep to realize that every letter in this book is placed with a purpose and cherish its developed characters.It's a story that revolves around a sibling which are Scout and Jem and their lives. Which gives unexpected shapes while living with their single parent which is their Father Atticus and caretaker Calpurnia. The Author uses emotions, struggles, hatred and fear during that time of history.

Tofuuu⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

This book has cured me of all of my terminal illnesses. The plot of this book is even better than the Inheritance Cycle, and has hooked me so well that I've been calling out of work sick simply so I can read this book.

FlyingBroomstick⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Honestly, this book is really amazing & unique from all the fiction books I've read. It was quite confusing in the start; first chapter was quite slow but as further I dig in, it kept going engaging and interesting. And the best thing is that it has so much profound characters & from them you can learn some great life lessons. The topics discussed in this book includes racial prejudice in America, struggle, injustice, cruelty and much more...there are times when it make you angry, make you laugh, make you smile and maybe make you cry in the end...I don't read books & novels at all... this was prob my 8th book and I would say the best so far...A must read book, I'd say!!
