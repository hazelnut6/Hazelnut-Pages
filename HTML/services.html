Our Services

At Hazelnut Pages, we offer a range of services to enhance your reading experience. Browse our services below:

  • Book Recommendations

    Our expert team of bookworms will recommend books based on your preferences, interests and reading history. Simply fill out our online form and we'll send you a list of books to read.

  • Book clubs

    Join one of our book clubs to discuss your favourite reads with like-minded individuals. We have clubs for all genres and interests, from sci-fi to romance to mystery.

  • Gift cards

    Surprise your loved ones with a Hazelnut Pages gift card. Our gift cards can be used to purchase any of our books or services.

  • Special orders

    Can't find the book you're looking for? We'll special order it for you. Just let us know the title and author, and we'll do the rest.

  • Free shipping

    We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. No need to leave the comfort of your own home to get your hands on your favourite books.
