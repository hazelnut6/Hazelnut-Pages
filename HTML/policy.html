Privacy Policy

Hazelnut Pages respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information, and your rights in relation to that information.

What information we collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as your name, email address, and payment information. We also collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and the items you view.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to provide you with our services, process your orders, and communicate with you about your orders and our products. We also use your information to improve our website and services, and to comply with legal requirements.

How we share your information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us provide our services, such as payment processors and shipping providers. We may also share your information with law enforcement agencies or regulators if required by law.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal information and to restrict its use. If you have any questions or concerns about your personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
